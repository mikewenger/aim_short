AIM Dilution Screener

Public leading indicators of a coming offering — ATM facilities, cash runway, shelf status, offering history
← AIM Short Long Screener v1.0
What this is and isn't: every signal below is public — a filed 8-K, a filed 10-Q, a filed S-3. This ranks who's structurally likely to raise capital soon (thin cash, standing sale capacity, a habit of doing so), the same way a credit analyst ranks default risk from public financials. It is not, and cannot be, advance knowledge of a specific deal — trading on actual non-public knowledge of a pending offering is insider trading.
