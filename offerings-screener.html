What this finds: companies that have already priced a public offering — a 424B3/424B4/424B5 prospectus filed with the SEC, which covers both IPOs and follow-on/secondary raises by already-public companies. This is the reactive counterpart to the Dilution Risk screener, which looks for offerings before they're announced.
